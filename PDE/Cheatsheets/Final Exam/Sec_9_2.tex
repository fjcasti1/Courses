We check whether any weak solution is given by the variation of parameters formula (9.17). Fix $t > 0$ and $n \in \N$ and $x \in \R$. Choose $\phi(y,s) = \Gamma (t-s+(1/n), x-y)$. We take the limit as $n \ra \infty$ and obtain $u(x,t) =  \int_0^t \int_{\R} \Gamma(t-s, x-y) f(y,s) dy ds$. This shows that any weak solution necessarily has this form.
